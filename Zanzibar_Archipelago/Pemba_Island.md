> **Pemba Island** (Arabic: الجزيرة الخضراء, al-Jazīra al-khadrāʔ; Swahili: Kisiwa cha Pemba) 
> is a Tanzanian island forming part of the Zanzibar Archipelago, 
> lying within the Swahili Coast in the Indian Ocean.
